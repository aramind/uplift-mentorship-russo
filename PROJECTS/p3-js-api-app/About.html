About Amihan

All About Air

Hi, I’m Russell Ferrero, and I created Amihan, an AI-powered web app designed to help people understand the air they breathe. This project was born out of my growing concern about air pollution and how it silently affects our health, our environment, and even our future.

We often take clean air for granted. But in many parts of the world, the air we breathe contains harmful pollutants that can cause respiratory illnesses, trigger allergies, and worsen existing health conditions. I wanted to build something that could make people more aware, something that turns raw data into meaningful insights, so we can all take small steps toward a healthier lifestyle and a cleaner environment.

Amihan empowers that awareness. It’s powered by real-time air quality data from IQAir, one of the world’s most trusted air quality platforms, and by Google Gemini AI, which interprets those numbers in a way that’s easy to understand, explaining what the data means, how it might affect you, and what actions you can take to stay safe.

Beyond numbers and maps, this project represents my belief that technology should be used for good, not just to make life easier, but to make life better. By combining reliable environmental data with artificial intelligence, Amihan encourages people to be more mindful of the environment and the invisible factors that impact our well-being every day.

Climate change isn’t something that happens far away, it’s happening right here, right now. And I believe that real change starts with awareness. Even small acts, choosing cleaner transport, supporting green initiatives, reducing waste, all add up. This web app is my way of contributing to that movement: by spreading knowledge, awareness, and a bit of inspiration.

Amihan, Powered by IQAir and Google Gemini. Built with passion, code, and a deep respect for the planet we all share.
